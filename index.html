Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 29, 2025

Welcome to QR Master ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience while using our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information that identifies you specifically. Our app operates primarily on your device.

  • Camera Data: We use your device's camera solely for the purpose of scanning QR codes and barcodes. No image data is sent to external servers for processing; all scanning happens locally on your device.
  • Storage Data: We may access your device storage to save generated QR codes or to scan images from your gallery, but only with your explicit permission.

2. How We Use Your Information

The limited data we access is used strictly for the app's core functionality:

  • To scan and decode QR codes and barcodes.
  • To generate and save new QR codes to your device.
  • To maintain a local history of your scans (which is stored only on your device).

3. Data Security

Since we do not collect personal data or transmit your scan history to cloud servers, your data remains secure on your personal device. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your own device.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., when you scan a URL). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party sites.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
